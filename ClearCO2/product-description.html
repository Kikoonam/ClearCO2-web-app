Pizza Margharita

Catogory: Food>Main Dish>Pizza

Pizza, dish of Italian origin consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with some combination of olive oil, oregano, tomato, olives, mozzarella or other cheese, and many other ingredients, baked quickly and served hot.

Components
  • ¾ cup and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup water
  • ¼ (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • ¼ pinch white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon flour for dusting
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 5 slices fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 5 leaves fresh basil
  • olive oil
  • sea salt to taste

Process Description

Step 1

Stir flour and 1 teaspoon salt in a bowl. Set aside.

Step 2

Mix water, yeast, and sugar in a large bowl. Let stand until yeast begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 minutes.
